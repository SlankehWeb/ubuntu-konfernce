the future of robotics

ROS is powering the future of robotics in industry, in the enterprise and for developers Ubuntu has been the primary platform for ROS from the very beginning, thanks to its flexibility and user-friendliness. ROS is led by Open Robotics, similar to how Canonical supports Ubuntu; Open Robotics steers the ship but it is driven by the community. As the robotics industry grows, companies and developers continue turning to Open Robotics and Canonical to help make their vision a reality.
